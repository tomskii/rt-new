5. Add children to your membership

You can apply to add any of your children to your Registered Traveller membership.

Your children can only use this service if they travel with the parent who applied for them.

Any changes to your membership will also apply to your child’s membership. For example, if your membership expires, your child’s membership will expire at the same time.

You won’t be able to use the ePassport gates when you travel with your children.

When you travel together, you and your children:

  • can use the UK and EEA passport lanes
  • don’t have to fill in a landing card

Eligibility

Your child must:

  • be aged 17 or under
  • have an eligible passport
  • have a visa, entry clearance or have visited the UK at least 4 times in the last 24 months

Fee

You’ll pay an administration fee of £20 for each child, plus £2 per month until the date your membership expires.

After you sign in and have entered your child’s date of birth, you’ll be told how much you need to pay.

You’ll get the membership fee back if your application is unsuccessful. You won’t get the £20 administration fee back.

How to apply

You must apply and pay for each child separately.

You’ll need:

  • your Registered Traveller number
  • your child’s passport

You’ll get a decision on your application within 10 working days.

If your application is successful, the first time you all travel to the UK you must:

  • go through the ‘other passports’ lane with your children
  • fill in a landing card for them

You may also need to prove the relationship between you and any children travelling with you, for example if you have a different surname.

You can do this with:

  • a birth or adoption certificate showing your relationship with the child
  • a divorce or marriage certificate if you’re the parent but have a different surname to the child

The immigration officer will then confirm if your child has been added to your membership. You’ll get a confirmation email within 48 hours.
